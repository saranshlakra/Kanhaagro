Eucalyptus Honey

One of the most distinctive variations of honey displaying a unique colour, delightful taste and strong fragrance is Eucalyptus Honey. The honey is extracted from pollinating a Eucalyptus flower (a mono-floral), eucalyptus provides an amber tone, sometimes green or darkish tones. A blessing for our respiratory system, Eucalyptus honey is often characterized as the most loved honey.

Wildflower Honey

Wildflower honey is extracted after the pollination of a huge selection of blossoms and flowers (poly-floral). Dark coloured and bold flavoured, WIldflower honey gives you a random variation of the local flora. The honey’s flavour and aroma is dependent on what wild honeybees prefer that season.

Mustard Honey

Mustard Honey is collected from a mono-floral nectar of the mustard flower. The Mustard Plant is produced for the mustard seeds, widely used in oil products, and sauces & spices production. With a dark yellow tone and oily texture, Mustard honey is moderately sweet.

Sunflower honey

As known as the traditional honey, Sunflower Honey also a mono-floral honey extracted from the sunflower’s bright yellow nectar providing the same color and mild sweetness. Thought, Sunflower honey has a creamy texture with a fine and delicious taste.

Litchi Honey

Litchi honey is captured after the pollination done by bees on a litchi tree (mono-floral). The honey has great gastric remedy qualities and is also helpful in regulating circulation of blood. It also displays neuro-enhancement tendancies which makes it a good dose to treat cognitive disorders. Litchi Honey is sweet in taste and easy to spread.
